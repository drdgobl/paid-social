LinkedIn

The only platform where we can target by job title, seniority, company, and industry. Home for B2B and high-consideration sales, if the budget can handle the premium.

Best for

B2B Professional services High-ticket B2C Account-based marketing Thought leadership Recruiting

What we run

  • Single image Sponsored Content
  • Document ads: strong for thought leadership and lead gen
  • Video ads
  • Lead Gen Forms: native, pre-filled from the profile
  • Conversation ads: high intent, use sparingly

How we target

  • Professional attributes: title, seniority, function, skills, industry
  • Company attributes: size, revenue, named-account lists for ABM
  • Matched Audiences from your contacts and site visitors
  • Interests layered on top

The honest tradeoff

StrengthTargeting that exists nowhere else, and leads that convert at 2 to 3x the rate of other platforms.
Watch outThe most expensive platform by far. Judge it on pipeline and cost-per-opportunity, not CPC.
