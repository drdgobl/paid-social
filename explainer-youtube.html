YouTube

Reaches nearly everyone through video and runs on Google's data. Strong for awareness and consideration when you have video worth watching.

Best for

Brand awareness Storytelling Product demos Search-intent reach

What we run

  • Skippable in-stream: the workhorse for most goals
  • Bumper ads: 6 seconds, awareness and reach
  • Shorts ads: vertical, UGC-style, mobile-first
  • Demand Gen: Google's AI across Shorts, in-stream, in-feed, Gmail

How we target

  • In-Market & Custom Segments from Google search terms (the unique edge)
  • Affinity audiences and demographics
  • Your Data / Customer Match for retargeting
  • Placements, keywords, topics for where ads show

The honest tradeoff

StrengthEnormous reach plus search intent no social platform has. Shorts inventory is cheap and effective.
Watch outLives in Google Ads (a separate skillset), weaker direct-sale attribution, and about five seconds to earn attention.
