Creative Energy · Paid Social

Creative Energy's take on paid social success.

Paid social is the most precise targeting and measurement in marketing, but it only works when it is built right. We keep a deliberately small, sharp menu so we can be genuinely good at what we offer. Three things make any campaign work: a budget big enough to optimize, a steady stream of fresh creative, and continuous optimization once it is live. Get those right and the platforms' AI does the heavy lifting of finding your buyers.

Three things that make paid campaigns work
Budget to optimize
Enough spend for the algorithm to learn and make a real impact. A one-off $500 push is not worth it.
Fresh creative
A steady stream of varied content so the audience never sees the same ad over and over.
Continuous optimization
No set it and forget it. We change course based on real performance, not opinions.
